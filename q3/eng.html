Collaborative Project: "The Greatest Sound"



This project contains a short story I made. It's about a man who's goal was to make a good quality headphones.


A draft of this work is available down below. But in case you didn't have the time, you can read this quick summary I made.


Settings:

  • Gren Residenses, 1989-2010
  • MIT University, 2007-2009
  • TSCC 2010, 2010
  • TC Manufacturing, 2010
  • The Federal Court, 2020
  • Gren Residenses, 2035

Characters:

  • David Gren, co-founder, Audia Technologies
  • Nicole Alexa Prendon-Gren, co-founder, Audia Technologies
  • David Gren's unnamed parents
  • Marco Sebastian, co-founder, The Quick Bits
  • Albert Wu, head of TC Manufacturing
  • Minlin, assistant of Mr. Wu

Conflict:

  • David Gren, co-founder of Audia Technologies wanted to make a good quality headphones at a lower costs, but he faces with a lot of obstacles and challenges he has to battle.

Plot

  • David Gren was born in 1989/li>
  • His parents saw that he has great interest in sound.
  • David met Nica at middle school, making them close friends.
  • He joined MIT by a school competiton.
  • He makes a speakers, but failed to present.
  • He and Nica dropped out of MIT to pursue theit dreams.
  • They make a successful product after 27 attempts.
