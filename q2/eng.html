English: Critical Listening and Taking Notes



Listening is part of the five senses; For me, listening is important. Which one is a fact?


Listening to something is great. And it also plays a big role to our lives. Listening for a topic requires your concentration, open-mindedness and understanding. Without any of those, you might misunderstand what the other person is trying to convey.


If you’re having a difficulty to listen, try one of these tips:


  • Try to take notes.
  • Listen to the key points.
  • Try to repeat what the person’s words.

Taking notes is also important. This will help you analyze or go deep to the information from the speech.


When taking notes, always write a short summary. You don’t have to write each part of the speech unless you’re taking notes of a trial.


Just make sure when listening and writing notes, know the facts and opinions before believing the one’s speech.
