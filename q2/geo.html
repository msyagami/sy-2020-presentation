Geometry: Pythagorean Theorem

Zhongli Genshin GIF from Zhongli GIFs

You probably heard Pythagorean Theorem a lot. Well, for certain reasons, it’s very crucial.

‎‎‎‏‏‎Common application of the Pythagorean Theorem is present in Architecture and Engineering. If you only need to find out the diagonal length of the roof.‎

‎‎‎‏‏‎Another application is in Navigation. You can use it and two lengths to find the shortest distance. For instance, if you are at sea and navigating to a point that is 300 miles north and 400 miles west, you can use the theorem to find the distance from your ship to that point and calculate how many degrees to the west of north you would need to follow to reach that point.

‎‎‎‏‏‎Other applications can be in Computer Science, and Physics.

Here's an example.

You drove to This Ave. for 400 meters north, and you turned left and travelled to That St. for 600 meters. Find the distance travelled.

‎‎‎‏‏‎\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\) will be our formula‎

Calculating it would look like this.

‎‎‎‏‏‎\(600^2 + 400^2 = c^2\)

Now let's simplify all terms.

‎‎‎‏‏‎‎‎‎‏‏‎\(360000 + 160000 = c^2\)

\(520000 = c^2\)

‎‎‎‏‏‎\(\sqrt{520000} = \sqrt{c^2}\)‎

Voila! You found the distance travelled!

‎‎‎‏‏‎It's\(~-721.11m\)!‎
